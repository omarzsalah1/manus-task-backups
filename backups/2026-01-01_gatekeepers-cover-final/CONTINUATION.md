## üé® DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### **Color Palette**
- **Background:** Muted sage-forest green (#3D4F3D to #4A5548)
- **Gold:** Antique brass (#B8962E to #A6894D) with warm ochre/brown undertones
- **Accent:** Cream (#F5F0E6) for barcode background

- **Sans-serif:** Cinzel, extended letter-spacing for "THE", "SOCIETY", "SALAH"

## üí° KEY LEARNINGS & BEST PRACTICES

1. **Iterative Refinement:** The design went through ~30+ iterations to achieve the perfect "antiquarian" aesthetic
2. **Color Psychology:** Moving from bright gold to aged brass was critical for achieving "quiet luxury"
3. **Typography Hierarchy:** Extended letter-spacing on sans-serif elements creates presidential gravitas
4. **Texture Matters:** Visible linen weave at 40-50 threads per inch is essential for authenticity
5. **Symbolism:** Clock at 5:47 (9/11 moment) adds powerful historical resonance
6. **Client Feedback:** Multiple rounds of typo corrections emphasized importance of proofreading
