# ESOTERIC PRESIDENTIAL TECHNOLOGY RESEARCH

## CRITICAL INSTRUCTION
This research requires **hyper-specific, esoteric, radical, and little-known technology details** about U.S. Presidential communications and personal technology throughout history.

**❌ DON'T give us:** "Air Force One has phones" (obvious)
**✅ DO give us:** "The NSA-modified BlackBerry with camera removed, model Sectera Edge, AES-256 encryption..."

## RESEARCH TARGETS

### Eisenhower Era
Not "car phone" but: exact Motorola model, weight (47 lbs?), VHF frequency, relay system specs, installation cost vs. civilian car prices

**Example of what we want:**
> "The Motorola RV-25 weighing forty-seven pounds in the trunk of his Chrysler Imperial, connected via VHF relay to a Pentagon switchboard, allowing John Foster Dulles to interrupt Ike's backswing at Burning Tree with news that Nasser had nationalized the Suez Canal. Installation cost $1,400—roughly what a new Chevrolet cost in 1956."

### LBJ Era
Not "liked phones" but: bathroom phone brand, waterproofing, how many buttons on his console, direct dial list by name

### Nixon Era
Not "taped conversations" but: Sony model numbers (TC-800B?), microphone count/placement, voice-activation trigger specs

### Obama Era
Not "kept BlackBerry" but: exact modified model (Sectera Edge? Custom RIM 8830?), which features removed, encryption standard, who could contact him (exact number of people)

### Trump Era
Which iPhone model, documented security failures, what apps were on it

### Biden Era
Peloton modifications required (camera disabled? Air-gapped? Secure room?)
