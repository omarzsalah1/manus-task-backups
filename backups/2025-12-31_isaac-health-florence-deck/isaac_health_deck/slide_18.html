The 90-Day Pilot Path

De-Risked Implementation with Measurable Milestones

Phase 1: Foundation
Weeks 1-4
  • Kickoff and discovery
  • Clinical workflow mapping
  • Integration planning
  • Pilot cohort selection (500-1,000 patients)
Phase 2: Integration & Testing
Weeks 5-8
  • EHR connection and ADT feeds
  • Sandbox testing
  • RCT training on Florence workflows
  • Escalation protocol validation
Phase 3: Scale Through ROI
Weeks 9-16
  • Expand to 5K+ members
  • Quality measure impact analysis
  • Readmission rate tracking
Phase 4: Full Integration
Week 17+
  • Universal deployment
  • Ongoing optimization
  • Quarterly business reviews
