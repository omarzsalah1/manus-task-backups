Florence Supports the Caregiver, Too

Caregiver burnout detection and proactive intervention—before crisis

1
DETECT
  • Sleep disruption: "I've been up every night this week"
  • Social isolation: "I haven't left the house in days"
  • Emotional exhaustion: "I just can't do this anymore"
  • Physical symptoms: "My back is killing me from lifting"
2
SCREEN
  • Zarit Burden Interview (short form)
  • PHQ-2 depression screening
  • Caregiver strain index assessment
  • Pattern analysis across multiple calls
3
ACT
  • Alert care navigator with burnout risk score
  • Respite care coordination (GUIDE requirement)
  • Support groups via Alzheimer's Assoc.
  • 24/7 support line access
Real Scenario: Linda Chen (Caregiver for mother with moderate dementia)

Week 1: "Mom's been wandering at night. I'm exhausted."

Week 2: "I had to quit my part-time job. I can't leave her."

Week 3: "I snapped at her yesterday. I feel terrible."

Florence Action: Flagged burnout risk (score: 8/10)

Care Navigator Alert: "Linda Chen showing signs of caregiver burnout. Recommend respite care discussion."

Outcome: Respite care arranged within 5 days; Linda reports improved sleep and mood. Returned to part-time work within 3 weeks.
