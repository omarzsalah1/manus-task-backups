How Florence Works—5-Step Clinical Workflow

Automated Care Management with Human Oversight

01
Read
Ingest ADT alerts, real-time clinical data via TEFCA/QHIN. Complete clinical context before first contact.
02
Triage
Apply Schmitt-Thompson protocols. Condition-specific assessment (dementia, CHF, COPD).
03
Teach-Back
SMS/voice adaptive outreach. Confirm member understanding.
04
Escalate
RN handoff when thresholds met. Full conversation history and clinical assessment.
05
Write
Structured notes to EHR. HEDIS-compliant documentation.
RN Escalation Thresholds
Temp >100.4°F, pain uncontrolled, low/no meds at home, suicidal ideation, unable to schedule follow-up, rapid cognitive decline.
