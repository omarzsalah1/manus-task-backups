CMS ACCESS Model - Key Facts for Isaac Health Deck Enhancement

SOURCE: https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/innovation-models/access

KEY FACTS:
- ACCESS = Advancing Chronic Care with Effective, Scalable Solutions Model
- Tests outcome-aligned payment approach in Original Medicare
- Expands access to technology-supported care options
- 10-year model beginning July 5, 2026
- Applications due April 1, 2026 for first performance period
- Applications after April 1, 2026 considered for January 1, 2027 start

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE:
- More than 500 technology-enabled care organizations have submitted intent to apply
- Leading medical societies have expressed support

CONDITIONS COVERED:
- High blood pressure
- Diabetes
- Chronic musculoskeletal pain
- Depression
(Affects more than two-thirds of people with Medicare)

KEY POINTS FROM CMS:
- Problem: Limited access to technology-supported care services due to Medicare payment barriers
- Solution: New payment option emphasizing outcomes over activities
- Enables clinicians to offer innovative technology-supported care
